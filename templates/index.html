ECO-PROGNOSIS: Empowering a Sustainable Future Through Precise Climate Prognosis

Eco- Prognosis aims to address the limitation of traditional climate models by harnessing the power of machine learning algorithm for climate prediction.
Applying machine learning allows us to increase the accuracy and speed of making these predictions .
Eco- Prognosis seeks to provide more accurate and reliable forecasts of temperature , precipitation , wind patterns, and other relevant climate variables.
By developing Eco-Prognosis we can significantly contribute to advancing climate predictin capabilities , enabling better decision-making , and aiding in climate change mitigation and adaptation efforts
